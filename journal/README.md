# Journal

The most important folder in this workspace. Every session, backtest, paper day, and lesson lands here as a dated entry.

> "The traders building working AI tools are not the ones with the most coding experience. They're the ones who figured out that the human's job is to make the AI's guesses better through planning, specific instructions, live context, and a system that remembers." — [../PRINCIPLES.md](../PRINCIPLES.md)

## Naming

- Daily entry: `YYYY-MM-DD.md`
- Backtest review: `YYYY-MM-DD-backtest-<strategy>.md`
- Lesson / pattern: `YYYY-MM-DD-lesson-<slug>.md`
- Weekly review: `YYYY-MM-DD-week.md` (Friday after close by default)

## Template (daily)

See [_template-daily.md](_template-daily.md). Copy it, rename with today's date, fill in.

## Template (weekly)

See [_template-weekly.md](_template-weekly.md). Use Friday after close by default.

## Why this exists

- **Compound memory.** Future-me reads past-me.
- **Pattern detection.** Losing trades that look random one at a time have a pattern across 30 of them.
- **Emotional discipline.** Writing "I overrode the rules because I was scared" once is enough to never do it again.
- **Strategy debugging.** When a strategy drifts from its backtest, the journal is where the clue lives.

## Rules

- Entries do not get deleted. Ever.
- Bad days are the most important entries.
- Write the entry BEFORE modifying any strategy code.
- Date entries in local time, note timezone in the file.
